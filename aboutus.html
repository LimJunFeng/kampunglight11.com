About Us

History of Kampung Kitchen Delight

Founded in the year 1971 by visionary Mark Lim, Kampung Kitchen Delight has become a beloved institution in Malaysia, dedicated to preserving the rich tapestry of traditional Malaysian cuisine. With an unwavering commitment to authenticity, the restaurant sources the finest local ingredients and employs time-honored techniques, ensuring that each dish is a masterpiece. Beyond the food, the rustic decor and welcoming staff create an ambiance reminiscent of a traditional Malaysian village, making every guest feel at home. Kampung Kitchen Delight has also emerged as a hub for culinary enthusiasts, offering workshops, events, and collaborations with local artisans. Today, it stands as a testament to Mark Lim's vision, not just as a restaurant, but as a cornerstone of Malaysia's culinary heritage, drawing food lovers from around the world.


Our Mission

We are on a mission to celebrate the diverse and flavorful cuisine of Malaysia. Through our dedication to authenticity and quality, we aim to transport our guests to the heart of Malaysian culture with every bite.

At Kampung Kitchen Delights, we believe that food is not just sustenance, but a celebration of heritage and community. We strive to create an experience that leaves a lasting impression on every guest who walks through our doors.

Insiparation

Nasi Lemak

Nasi Lemak is a popular Malaysian dish consisting of fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaf, served with anchovies, peanuts, boiled egg, cucumber, and sambal.

Asam Laksa

Asam Laksa is a sour fish-based soup commonly found in Malaysia. It is made with poached fish, tamarind, and a mixture of herbs and spices.

Nasi Kerabu

Nasi Kerabu is a traditional Malay dish known for its blue-colored rice. It is served with various fresh herbs, vegetables, and a choice of protein.
